Personal Growth Accordion

What is the meaning of life?
The meaning of life is something we each create for ourselves. It’s about finding purpose in the things we love, helping others, and learning from every experience we have.
What does happiness mean to you?
Happiness isn’t a constant feeling — it’s peace within yourself. For me, it’s about being grateful, accepting what I can’t control, and enjoying simple moments.
How do you define success in your life?
Success means living a life that feels true to who I am. It’s not about comparison or perfection, but about progress, growth, and balance.
What is your biggest fear, and how do you face it?
My biggest fear is not living up to my potential. I try to face it by taking small steps every day, even when I’m uncertain or scared.
